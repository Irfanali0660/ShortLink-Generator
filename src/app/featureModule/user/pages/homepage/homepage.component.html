What is shortGen ?

ShortGen is a all-in-one Link Management Platform for all your links and needs. It is an advanced URL Shortener with extensive Link Analytics. ShortGen offers solutions that will allow you to manage your links, thanks to which you can develop your business while having your links under control. ShortGen is also a platform for creating your own Link in bio microsites and generating QR Codes, so you can combine offline and online solutions.
